    Filter Target Entities

    Advanced Search: filter the target entities' relationships with other entities with a certain condition.
             eg.Filter the SKU which has relationship with document whose "Asset type" is "warning label".

    Filter Button: filter the entity which meets the certain condition.(S1: click the filter button. S2: Type the condition in the search bar.)
             eg. Find the SKU which is end of purchase.
    Disable/Enable Filter: Untick or tick the checkbox to disable or enable the filter.
    Pin Filter: Pin the attribute you frequently filter.(Only can filter 10 attributes at most)
    Unpin Filter: Unpin the attribute that you temporarily do not need to use.
    Remove Filter: Remove the filter that you no longer need.

    Download Entities

    S1: Select entities you want to download by ticking the checkbox.
    S2: Click the download button.("Download in Excel":50,000 entities at most;"Download in CSV":500,000 entities at most.)
    S3: Click "Select Attributes" to search the attributes you want to download and move them to the right column.
         Also, you can click "Select Relationships" to choose the relationships you want to download and move them to the right column.
    S4: Click "Download" to download the selected attributes and one entity's relationships with other entities.
    S5: Click "Show Tasks Status" and wait for the tasks to complete.
             eg.Download 20 SKUs'"Update Attributes from Sourcer","CM Latest Confirm Date"and their relashionships with document.

    Create/Delete a Saved Search

    There are two places for you to create saved searches: Search Page; Download Page.
    Search page:
    1. Create Saved Search:
         S1: Filter the condtions you frequently use.
         S2: Click on the drop-down button on the "Serch Thing Domain".
         S3: Click "Create or Edit Saved Search".
         S4: Name a name for you saved search and Save it.
         S5: Next time, you can find the saved search you created in "My Search" in the bottom field of Home Page.
         eg. Create a saved search for SKU which "Repeating Order Status" is "Need Document" and name this saved search as "Need document".
    2. Delete Saved Search: Click the rubbish button on the saved search you want to delete.

    Download page:
    1. Create Saved Search:
         S1: Choose the attributes you want to save in the saved search and move them to the right column.
         S2: Name a name for you saved search and Save it.
         S3: Next time, you can find your saved search in the "My Scope Selections".
    2. Delete Saved Search: Click the rubbish button on the saved search you want to delete.
